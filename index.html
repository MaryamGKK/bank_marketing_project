Optimizing Bank Marketing

A Data-Driven Calling Strategy to Maximize ROI

The Challenge: Efficiency Under Pressure

With over 41,000 historical calls and limited resources, how can a bank maximize term deposit subscriptions? The key is to stop calling randomly and start predicting who is most likely to say "yes".

The Strategy: Predictive Prioritization

Instead of random outreach, we built a system to rank every potential customer by their probability of subscribing. This simple, powerful rule transforms the campaign's efficiency.
